Volunteer

The Church of Grant is always looking for volunteers. Volunteering for the Church is a great way to help make the world a better place! Stop in the Church building to sign up for one of these incredible volunteering opportunities.

Nursery Helper

At our Friday night services, lots of people bring their children to our nursery while they bask in Grant's Glory. We need some volunteers to help take care of the children during this time!

Grant's Personal Housekeeper

What better way to serve the Church by being a housekeeper for the Prophet Grant himself! His mansion constantly needs cleaning and upkeep, and he is looking for volunteers to help him out.

Chicken Cooker

This is one of the most honorable and best things you can do for the Church. Chicken is served at every Friday night service and we need some volunteers to help cook the chicken and serve it.
