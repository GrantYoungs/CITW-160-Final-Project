The Church of Grant loves and welcomes all!

Welcome to the Church of Grant! We would love to have you at our Friday night services and join us in worship for our leader and founder, the Prophet Grant.

Services are Friday nights from 7:30 PM to 8:30 PM. Free coffee and teriyaki chicken is served at each service. Please come and join us!
